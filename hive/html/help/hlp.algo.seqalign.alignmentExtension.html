hlp.algo.seqalign.alignmentExtension Alignment extension Specify the mode of alignment extension This option allows the user to specify how the alignment will be extended. The Extended option continues aligning the sequences until it reaches the threshold of mismatches encountered, resulting in lower sensitivity but higher coverage. The Compact option terminates the alignment as soon as a higher score is obtained, providing higher sensitivity but lower coverage. The compact mode is a good choice when working with small genomes that have a higher mutation rate.
