hlp.obj.svc-profiler.tab-gapsandcontigs.html Gaps and Contigs View position and coverage of reported gaps and contigs This table displays information about all reported gaps and contigs as computed based on coverage and input parameters for the SNP profiler tool. Columns are as follows:
  • # is an arbitrary number assigned to regions in consecutive order.
  • Start is the starting position on the reference genome to which the gap or contig maps.
  • End is the end position of the gap/contig with respect to the reference genome position.
  • Length is the length of the corresponding gap or contig.
  • Average Coverage sums the depth of coverage across all nucleotides in the region and divides this quantity by the total length.
  • Has Coverage tells whether the corresponding region has any coverage (true) or not (false).
