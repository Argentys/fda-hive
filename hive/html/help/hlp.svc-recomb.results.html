hlp.svc-recomb.results.html Recombination profile results Interpret viewers and download graphs resulting from recombination analysis
    Once computation is complete, results can be viewed in the Recombination Profile viewer at the bottom of the page. Results can be viewed as individual or superimposed plots of coverage across multiple references, depending on the specific references selected. To select references for the results view, click the corresponding checkboxes in the "Recombination Algorithm Parameters" input section at the top of the page.
