hlp.algo.smithWatermanOptimizations Smith-Waterman Optimizations Choose whether to compute a diagonal of specified length or to compute the entire substitution matrix This parameter is used to optimize the Smith-Waterman algorithm by computing only the highly probable alignment diagonal. The width determines the seed length. Greater seed length or width is more expensive to compute but has a potential of constructing high scoring alignments with larger gaps and mismatches. Smaller values make the search faster but have a potential of missing longer gaps, thus diminishing the gapped alignment sensitivity. Setting this parameter to “auto” tells the algorithm to choose the value equal to the length of the seed.
