hlp.view.alignment.histogram.html Alignment histogram help How to interpret the graph
  • Unaligned Reads: number of reads that successfully aligned, classified by read length
  • Aligned Reads: number of reads that failed to align, also classified by their read length
  • Alignments: number of those parts of reads which successfully aligned, classified by the length of the part that aligned — which may be smaller than the length of the whole read.
If the distribution of Alignments and Aligned Reads differ, this typically indicates a QA problem.
